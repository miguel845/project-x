T's Photography Brand

The name T's Photography was inspired by the founders original first name Teflon Migos back in his youth years. It was his dream to indulge into this photography business, thus buiding his own photography brand from scratch. In order to achieve this, he needed a team to work with hence recruiting a team consisting of four members. He was able to successfully recruit a team that met his criteria and desired charaters on an individual level as well. It was with this team that he was able to slowly but sure make his dream a reality.
